BIET Bhadrak

BIET Overview

With the prime aim to provide qualitative technical education in the state of Odisha, Bhadrak Institute of Engg & Tech. was set up in the year 1997 ushering in a new era of technical education. Situated away from the madding crowd amidst the beauty and bounty of nature at Barapada, Bhadrak, the place is truly an abode of excellence. Spread over a sprawling 100 acres of land, the institute offers undergraduate and post graduate programmes like B.Tech, MBA & MCA. The state of the art infrastructure, experienced & dedicated Faculties, highly sophisticated laboratories and above all the enviable academic record makes it ranked among the top Technical Institutions of Odisha.

BIET Bhadrak invites interested candidates to apply through JoSAA / CSAB 2024 for admission to B.Tech. Programme in 15 different branches, B.Arch. Programme, Integrated M.Sc. (5 years) Programme in 04 different branches, B.Tech. and M.Tech Dual Degree Programme in 04 branches for the academic year 2024-25.
